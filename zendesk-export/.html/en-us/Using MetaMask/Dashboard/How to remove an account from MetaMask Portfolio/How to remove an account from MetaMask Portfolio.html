Looking for how to remove an account in MetaMask?

This article is about removing accounts from MetaMask Portfolio, at portfolio.metamask.io. For information on removing accounts in MetaMask, see here.

Head to Settings > Accounts.

Now, find the account you want to remove and click the three vertical dots next to it to access the 'More actions' menu.

In this menu, simply hit 'Remove account'. You'll be prompted to confirm. When you do, it'll be removed from your dashboard.Â
