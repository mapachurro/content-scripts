For an automatic process, go to Chainlist, search for Gnosis, and connect your wallet.

 

For a manual process:

Follow the steps found here to add a custom network. You can also head here and click 'Add to MetaMask' in the top-right corner to quickly add it. 

If you need the custom network  details to add it manually, use:

  • Network Name: Gnosis Chain

  • New RPC URL: https://rpc.gnosischain.com 

  • Chain ID: 0x64 (the hexadecimal equivalent of 100; if you encounter issues, input 100 instead and retry)

  • Symbol: xDai

  • Block Explorer URL: https://blockscout.com/xdai/mainnet/
