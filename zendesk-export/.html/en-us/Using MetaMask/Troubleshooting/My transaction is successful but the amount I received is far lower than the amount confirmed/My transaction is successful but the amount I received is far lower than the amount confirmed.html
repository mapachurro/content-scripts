If you have completed a transaction successfully but you've noticed a discrepancy between the amount confirmed in your MetaMask wallet and the amount you received (and displayed in the block explorer), please check if the token you received is subject to a "tax".

As an example, a 10% tax applies on SAFEMOON transactions, and OKTA transactions are subject to an 8% tax.

For further details please refer to the token issuer documentation.
